Description of Service

Ellie AI provides an automated SMS receptionist service that replies to missed calls on behalf of small businesses. When a call goes unanswered, Ellie sends an automated text message to the caller on behalf of the business.

SMS Messaging Program

By calling a business that uses Ellie AI, you may receive automated SMS messages from that business via Ellie's platform. These messages are sent to help the business respond to your missed call inquiry.

  • Opt-out: Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent.
  • Opt-in: Reply START to resubscribe after opting out.
  • Help: Reply HELP for assistance or contact us at support@meetellieai.com
  • Message frequency: Varies based on business activity and your interactions.
  • Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.
