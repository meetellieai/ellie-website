Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Overview

Ellie AI ("we," "our," or "us") operates an AI-powered SMS receptionist service that automatically replies to missed calls on behalf of small businesses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our service.

By using Ellie, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Phone numbers: Mobile phone numbers of end users who call our clients' businesses and receive automated SMS replies.
  • Message content: The content of SMS conversations between end users and the Ellie AI assistant.
  • Business information: Information provided by our business clients including business name, contact details, and service descriptions.
  • Usage data: Information about how our service is used, including message timestamps and response metrics.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver automated SMS responses to missed calls on behalf of our business clients
  • Improve the accuracy and quality of our AI responses
  • Provide our business clients with conversation logs and analytics
  • Comply with legal obligations and carrier requirements

SMS Messaging & Consent

End users receive SMS messages from Ellie AI when they place a missed call to a business that uses our service. By calling a business that uses Ellie, end users provide implied consent to receive an automated SMS follow-up from that business.

End users may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. After opting out, no further messages will be sent. To resume messages, reply START. For help, reply HELP.

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on business activity.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information with:

  • Our business clients: Conversation data is shared with the business whose missed call triggered the SMS interaction.
  • Service providers: We use third-party services including Twilio (SMS delivery), Anthropic (AI processing), and Supabase (data storage) to operate our service. These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and do not use your data for independent marketing purposes.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

Data Retention

We retain conversation data for up to 90 days to provide our service and allow business clients to review interactions. After this period, data is deleted unless required by law.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of SMS communications by replying STOP
  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns
