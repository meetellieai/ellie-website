Automated SMS Follow-Up Disclosure

This page explains how and when Ellie AI sends automated SMS messages on behalf of businesses that use our service.

📋 How Consent Is Obtained

Ellie AI provides an automated SMS receptionist service for small trade businesses. When a customer calls a business that uses Ellie AI and the call goes unanswered, our system will automatically send an SMS follow-up message to that caller on behalf of the business.

By calling a business that uses Ellie AI, you acknowledge and agree that you may receive an automated SMS text message reply from that business. This constitutes your consent to receive such messages.

Businesses using Ellie AI are required to display this disclosure so that callers are informed prior to or at the time of calling. The act of placing a call to a business using Ellie AI serves as the opt-in trigger for this automated follow-up communication.

💬 What Messages You May Receive

If you call a business using Ellie AI and your call is not answered, you may receive an automated SMS message that:

• Acknowledges your missed call on behalf of the business
• Asks for basic information such as your name and the nature of your inquiry
• Helps schedule a callback or appointment
• Connects you with the business as quickly as possible

Messages are sent by Ellie AI on behalf of the business you called. Message frequency will vary based on your interaction. Message and data rates may apply.

🚫 How to Opt Out

You can opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time using the following keywords:

STOP

Reply STOP to immediately unsubscribe from all further messages.

HELP

Reply HELP to receive assistance or contact information.

After replying STOP, you will receive a single confirmation message and no further messages will be sent. You can re-subscribe at any time by replying START.

🔒 Your Privacy

Ellie AI does not sell, rent, or share your personal information or phone number with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information is used solely to facilitate the communication between you and the business you contacted.
